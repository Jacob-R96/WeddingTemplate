Flights

For flights the destination to fly into for the Wedding will be the Kahului Airport (OOG) in Maui. Things to consider when deciding the flights is the time shift from central time to Hawaian time. If its 1pm in Texas its 8am in Hawaii.
Another thing to consider is comfortability. This flight is a minimum to 6 hours in the air, so wear comfortable clothing, download ur favorite netflix series, and get a window seat.
Important:
Fly Into: Kahului Airport (OOG)
